My Recipies

Looking for quick comfort food recipes? These incredibly delicious mains and desserts can be on the table in less than an hour.

Air Fryer Salmon

For the fastest meal ever, turn to your air fryer. This salmon cooks in just 10 minutes and comes out perfectly tender on the inside with an amazing top crust. The sweet mustard topping makes this one of our favorite salmon dishes.

Zucchini Salad

It's hot and humid and you really don't want to cook: just the thought of turning on a burner boils your blood. Enter: zucchini salad, no cooking and no heat necessary. All you need is 30 to 40 minutes, and a zen state of mind.

French Pizza Bread

Skip the homemade pizza dough and grab the nearest French bread. The loaf makes for a base that has a crispy outside with a soft inside. It's the best of both worlds and ours gets brushed with garlic butter to combine our two favorite things – garlic bread and pizza. Top the pizza with any of your favorite toppings.
